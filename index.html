About iSAIL Lab

iSAIL(iSchool Statistical Machine Learning And Artificial Intelligence Lab), founded by Professor Jingrui He, is housed in the School of Information Sciences at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. Prof. He has been working on a wide range of topics related to artificial intelligence, machine learning and data mining, especially in heterogeneous machine learning, rare category analysis, active learning, and semi-supervised learning. Our group have been working on a wide range of research topics related, including but not limited to:

  • Rare Category Analysis
  • Adversarial Machine Learning
  • Heterogeneous Machine Learning, Transfer Learning
  • Graph Representation Learning, Graph Mining
