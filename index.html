About SDC Lab

SDC Lab(Self-Decision Control) Laboratory, founded by Professor Jingliang Duan, is housed in the School of Mechanical Engineering at the University of Science and Technology Beijing. Prof. Duan has been working on a wide range of topics related to reinforcement learning, optimal control, autonomous driving decisions and intelligent operation control.
